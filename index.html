How to play

At sea — W sail forward, S reverse, A/D steer. Steer toward a port and press E to land.

On foot — WASD walk, Shift run, mouse to look. Find the glowing monument and press E to read the town's history. Return to the dock and press E to set sail again.

On each island: witness the town's dramatic event, read its history at the glowing monument, find the floating 📜 letter, and look for special challenges:

• ✍ Write the epistle — at some ports you reorder the words of a real verse from Paul's letters.
• ☀ Spread the light — in some towns, walk up to the dark lamps to share the good news and fill the town with light.
